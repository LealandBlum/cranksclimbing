Lealand Blum

Lealand Blum is the cheif editor and runs Crank's blog. He grew up in the tiny town of Tooele, Utah. His roots go back the great white north of Alberta, Canada where his parents were born and raised.

He discovered a love for the outdoors just after he started college and began taking outdoor classes through Brigham Young University. He enjoys mountain biking, scuba diving, longboarding, roller blading, camping, and most of all rock climbing. He has been an avid climber for 3 years and is crushing 5.8's outdoors.

Just kidding. He has been climbing in the high 10's and low 11's during the last couple of months and is looking to push into the mid 11's by the end of the season. Pray for his finger's tendies. They'll need it...
